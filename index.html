About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at Boise State University specializing in cybersecurity, behavioral biometrics, VR/AR security, and brainwave-based authentication. My research focuses on enhancing the security of smart wearables, VR devices, and intelligent systems using AI-driven behavioral signals.

Research Areas

  • AI-driven Behavioral Biometrics
  • Brainwave Authentication (EEG-based security)
  • VR/AR Sensor Security & Data Integrity
  • Continuous User Authentication
  • Side-Channel Analysis
  • Privacy-Preserving Machine Learning
