About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Boise State University. My research focuses on cybersecurity, offensive and defensive analysis of computing devices, brainwave authentication, behavioral biometrics, and AI-driven user authentication for VR and emerging systems.

Research Group

Our group investigates multimodal biometric authentication, VR-based user verification, EEG/ERP-driven authentication, and adversarial analysis of machine learning models.

Research Areas

  • Behavioral Biometrics & Active Authentication
  • Brainwave Authentication using ERP signals
  • VR Security: hand/head motion modeling
  • AI-based threat modeling & signal processing

Collaboration Team

  • Graduate & Undergraduate Students (BSU)
  • Collaborators from URI, Datwyler, and partner labs
