Research Group

We conduct cybersecurity research to drive innovations in emerging technologies that protect identity, safeguard privacy, and enhance overall security. Specifically, we:

  • Leverage behavioral biometrics to identify and authenticate users based on unique behavior patterns, enabling continuous authentication and fraud detection.
  • Analyze multimodal data from wearables and other Internet of Things (IoT) devices to unveil vulnerabilities and risks.
  • Strengthen security in artificial intelligence (AI) and intelligent systems that emulate human decision-making.
  • Address privacy and security challenges in immersive virtual environments.
